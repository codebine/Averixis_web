Averixis Training Programme

Welcome to the Averixis Training Programme — where innovation meets opportunity. Our Training is designed to equip you with hands-on experience, working with top-tier professionals in areas such as AI, Web Development, Data Science, and UI/UX Design. From curated training to real-world project exposure, Averixis empowers the next generation of tech leaders.

Programme Overview

Gain practical exposure to industrial tools and environments. Our Training includes guided modules, weekly mentor check-ins, and hands-on assignments that align with industry needs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students in their 2nd year and above in relevant fields (CS, IT, Design, etc.).
  • Basic understanding of programming or design principles.
  • Passion to learn and solve problems creatively.

Project Domains

  • Web Development (Frontend + Backend)
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Mobile App Development
  • UI/UX Design and Prototyping

Training Benefits

  • Certificate of Completion
  • Letter of Recommendation for top performers
