Course Overview

We help undergraduate and postgraduate students struggling to get industrial experience with our Industry Grade Mentorship programs. Our training and internships are designed to make students corporate-ready with practical skills and confidence to take on real-world challenges.

Vision: We envision a world where every student receives effective, equitable, and engaging education to reach their full potential.

Mission: To be one of the most preferred education technology platforms across the globe, empowering students with in-demand tech skills.

Course Curriculum

  • Overview of app development & importance
  • Intro to programming languages: Java, Swift, Kotlin
  • Setup Android Studio or Xcode
  • UI/UX principles, layout design
  • Building front-end & interactive elements
  • Project: Build a simple app

  • Using sensors: Camera, GPS, etc.
