Internship Details

Internship Opportunities at Averixis

At Averixis, we believe in nurturing talent and fostering growth. Join us to gain real-world experience, mentorship, and make a meaningful impact.

Programme Overview

Gain practical exposure to industrial tools and environments. Our internship includes guided modules, weekly mentor check-ins, and hands-on assignments that align with industry needs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students in their 2nd year and above in relevant fields (CS, IT, Design, etc.).
  • Basic understanding of programming or design principles.
  • Passion to learn and solve problems creatively.

Project Domains

  • Web Development (Frontend + Backend)
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Mobile App Development
  • UI/UX Design and Prototyping

Internship Benefits

  • Certificate of Completion
  • Letter of Recommendation for top performers
  • Portfolio-worthy projects
  • Access to Averixis alumni network
