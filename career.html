EMPOWERING SMALL BUSINESSES

Our Mission

SinaLite was founded to Empower small business owners in the advertising and marketing sector to boost their sales and profitability. We strive to be a leader by offering high-quality marketing materials through ecommerce and lean manufacturing.

Who We Are

SinaLite offers flexible marketing solutions for business owners in the advertising and marketing sector within North America. With our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and our online technology, we produce and fulfill over five million orders each year.

In order to provide our clients with innovative solutions, we embrace new developments in e-commerce, manufacturing technology and marketing trends. And everyday, our talented team keeps SinaLite moving towards our company mission.

Beliefs

Our Culture

It is our team who makes SinaLite. We come from diverse backgrounds, but we share the same common goal: to empower small business owners in the advertising and marketing sector to boost their sales and profitability.

SinaLite encourages an open culture, where every team member is a hands-on contributor. We are proud to maintain an environment in which employees feel comfortable sharing ideas and suggestions.

Our team is made up of ambitious and talented individuals who are crucial to our continued success. While experience is an advantage for our candidates, we favour individuals who are capable and driven.
